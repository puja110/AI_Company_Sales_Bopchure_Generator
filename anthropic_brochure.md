# Welcome to Anthropic

## About Us
Anthropic is a public benefit corporation dedicated to advancing artificial intelligence in a way that prioritizes safety and reliability. Our mission is to build AI systems that serve humanity's long-term well-being. Founded in San Francisco, our diverse team comprises experts from fields such as physics, machine learning, public policy, and business. Together, we work as a cohesive unit to forecast and mitigate the potential risks of AI technologies.

## Our Culture
At Anthropic, we embrace a collaborative and inclusive company culture that values the well-being of every employee. We believe that supporting our team is essential to our collective success, which is why we offer extensive health and wellness benefits, a commitment to work-life balance, and a supportive environment that fosters innovation and collaboration.

### Employee Benefits
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision plans
- Flexible work arrangements to promote work-life balance
- Continuous learning and development opportunities through the Anthropic Academy

## Our Customers
We serve a diverse array of clients who leverage our AI systems to enhance productivity, streamline workflows, and drive innovation. Our flagship products, including **Claude**, **Claude Code**, and the **Claude Developer Platform**, are designed to meet the needs of both individual developers and large organizations.

## Our Products
- **Claude**: A leading AI system for coding, computer interactions, and various enterprise applications.
- **Claude Code**: Equipped with state-of-the-art features for coding assistance.
- **Claude Developer Platform**: A robust environment for developers looking to integrate advanced AI functionalities into their applications.

## Research and Transparency
We treat AI safety as a systematic science, conducting rigorous research into the safety, interpretability, and societal impacts of our AI models. Our **Transparency Hub** ensures that our processes and practices for responsible AI development are open to scrutiny, allowing us to build trust with our stakeholders.
