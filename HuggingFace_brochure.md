## About Us
Founded in 2016, Hugging Face is a pioneering AI company dedicated to democratizing artificial intelligence through the power of natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning. We provide a collaborative platform for developers and researchers, offering access to a wide array of models, datasets, and applications that empower innovation in AI technologies.

## Our Platform
At Hugging Face, users can explore over **1 million models** and over **250,000 datasets**. Our platform includes:

- **Models**: Access state-of-the-art machine learning models for various applications, with new models refreshed regularly.
- **Datasets**: Browse an extensive library of datasets to support research and development.
- **Spaces**: Create and share interactive applications powered by machine learning models.

We also offer **enterprise solutions** designed for organizations looking to train and scale their AI initiatives with advanced security, dedicated support, and comprehensive analytics.
